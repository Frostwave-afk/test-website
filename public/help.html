Getting Started Guide
1

Create your account or sign in

Visit the login page and create a student account using your college email and password. If you already have an account, simply sign in.

2

Fill in your personal information

On the form page, enter your name, phone number, gender, and languages spoken. All fields marked with * are required.

3

Rate your 10 subjects

Click the number (1–10) that best represents your rating for each subject. 1 = Poor, 10 = Excellent. All subjects must be rated.

4

Review and submit

Check all your details on the review screen. Click "Submit Form" when you're ready. You can always edit your responses later from the Home page.

5

Edit your submission

On the Home page, click the "Edit Form" button on the form card to update your ratings or personal information at any time before the deadline.
